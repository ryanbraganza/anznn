Common Unicode characters that cause problems in csv files:

• - Bullet (Substitution: *)
? - Unknown (displays as a "?" but isn't. Substitution: [?])
‘ - Open smart single quote (Substitution: ')
’ - Close smart single quote (Substitution: ')
“ - Open smart double quote (Substitution: '')
” - Close smart double quote (Substitution: '')
– - Em Dash (Substitution: -)
